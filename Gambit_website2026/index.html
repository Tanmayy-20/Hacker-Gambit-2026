Event Timeline

11 July, 2026

Registration Opens

Registrations officially open for Hackers Gambit 2026. Teams and individual participants from colleges, universities, and the cybersecurity community are invited to register for India's premier Capture The Flag competition. Early registration provides access to event updates, preparation resources, and important announcements.

26 September, 2026

Registration Closes

AThe registration portal closes at 11:59 PM IST. All registered participants will receive event credentials, Discord server access, competition guidelines, and the official rulebook. No registrations will be accepted after the deadline.

2 October, 2026 • 11:00 AM IST

Opening Ceremony

Hackers Gambit 2026 officially begins with a live inauguration featuring the organizing committee, sponsors, and special guests. Participants will receive an overview of the competition format, challenge categories, scoring system, platform walkthrough, and event rules before the competition begins.

2 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM IST

Online Qualifier Begins

The 48-hour online Capture The Flag officially starts. Participants from across the country compete in a wide range of cybersecurity challenges.Teams will race against time to earn points and secure a place in the Grand Finale.

4 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM IST

Online Qualifier Ends

After an intense 48-hour competition, the online round concludes. Final submissions are locked, leaderboards are frozen, and challenges are closed for evaluation. Teams must ensure all flags are submitted before the deadline.

4 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM IST

Write-up Submission Opens

To validate challenge-solving approaches and maintain fair competition, participating teams are required to submit detailed write-ups explaining their methodologies, exploitation techniques, and solutions for the completed challenges. The write-up submission portal remains open for two days.

6 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM IST

Top 15 Teams Announced

Following the evaluation process, the Top 15 teams qualifying for the Grand Finale are officially announced. Qualified teams receive invitations, travel information, reporting instructions, accommodation details (if applicable), and final event guidelines.

30 October, 2026 • 11:00 AM IST

Finalist Check-in & Registration

Finalist teams arrive at Jaihind College of Engineering, Kuran, complete on-site registration, receive participant kits, ID badges, event merchandise, and verify their systems before the competition begins.

30 October, 2026 • 11:30 AM IST

Grand Finale Briefing

Organizers conduct the final briefing covering competition rules, infrastructure setup, judging process, attack-defense guidelines (if applicable), safety protocols, and sponsor introductions before the official start.

30 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM IST

Grand Finale Begins

The 24-hour offline Capture The Flag Grand Finale begins. Finalist teams compete head-to-head in a high-pressure environment, solving advanced cybersecurity challenges while demonstrating technical expertise, teamwork, and strategic thinking to become the champions of Hackers Gambit 2026.

31 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM IST

Grand Finale Ends

The competition concludes after 24 hours of continuous hacking, analysis, and problem-solving. Final scores are verified, infrastructure is secured, and the judging committee prepares the official results.

31 October, 2026 • 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM IST

Score Verification & Networking

While the organizing team verifies final results, participants can interact with sponsors, industry professionals, faculty members, and fellow cybersecurity enthusiasts through networking sessions and refreshments.

31 October, 2026 • 1:00 PM IST

Awards & Closing Ceremony

Hacker Gambit 2026 concludes with the official closing ceremony. Winners are announced, trophies and cash prizes are presented, sponsor recognitions are delivered, participation certificates are distributed, and the event concludes with a group photograph celebrating the successful completion of India's premier student-led cybersecurity competition.
